# GDPR Principle Mapping

## 1. Data Minimization
Risk: Excessive indexing of internal documents in AI system.
Control: Restrict indexing to business-critical documents only.

## 2. Purpose Limitation
Risk: Reuse of customer KYC data for unrelated analytics.
Control: Explicit consent tagging and purpose documentation.

## 3. Integrity & Confidentiality
Risk: Unauthorized access to financial database.
Control: Role-based access + Multi-Factor Authentication.

## 4. Storage Limitation
Risk: Indefinite retention of employee HR records.
Control: Automated retention and deletion policies.

## 5. Accountability
Risk: Lack of audit trail for sensitive data access.
Control: Maintain access logs and quarterly audits.
